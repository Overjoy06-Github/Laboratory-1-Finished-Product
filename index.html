LABORATORY #1 - Final Output

Attack on Titan

Sypnosis

In a world overrun by massive humanoid creatures called Titans, humanity's last survivors live behind towering walls. When a colossal Titan breaches their defenses, young Eren Yeager vows revenge and joins the Survey Corps to fight back. Alongside his friends Mikasa and Armin, Eren uncovers dark secrets about the Titans, the world, and his own mysterious powers. As the battle for survival escalates, the truth behind the walls threatens to change everything.
